Our Mission

My Garden is a question and answer forum for experienced and new gardeners alike. It is the National Wildlife Federation's goal to create better global gardening community, and that starts with you! My Garden connects you with other gardeners who can answer your questions based on their experiences!

Feel free to ask a question or learn more about the N.W.F's Gardening for Wildlife Program.
